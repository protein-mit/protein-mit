Glossary




Exercises

  • BENCH (aka supine press): Bench press, the most important exercise
  • Crazy eights (aka loco ochos): Bicep workout - do 8 curls at the lowest weight possible (with dumbbells or fixed barbells), then immediately proceed to the next weight up and do another 8 reps. Continue until you cannot do a full 8 reps, then take a few-minute break and do 8 reps of the weight you failed at. Work your way down the rack (8 reps each weight without stopping) until complete.
  • Flaccid fives (aka flaccids): Crazy eights, but only 5 reps
  • Belly bounces: Front squats, holding the bar tucked in your elbows
  • Nipple blasting: Regular squats, so named as a cue to keep your chest pointed towards the ground

Equipment

  • Double Wagonwheels (aka Double Wagz, aka Dubz): Two 45 lb plates on each side of the bar (225 lbs total)
  • Buscuits: 2.5 lb plates. Disgusting
